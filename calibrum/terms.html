1. What Calibrum Is

Calibrum is an educational companion app for adult men using testosterone replacement therapy under the supervision of a licensed healthcare provider, including injectable testosterone (cypionate, enanthate, propionate, undecanoate / Aveed®), topical or oral testosterone (AndroGel®, Testim®, Fortesta®, Testopel®, Jatenzo®, Kyzatrex®), aromatase inhibitors (anastrozole, Arimidex®, Aromasin®), and adjuncts such as hCG (Pregnyl®, Novarel®). The app helps you log injections, rotate injection sites, decode LabCorp / Quest / hospital lab PDFs, triage estradiol-related symptoms, calculate Free Testosterone with the Vermeulen formula, time your bloodwork around dose changes, and prepare a summary for your endocrinology or urology visits. The "AI Coach" persona ("Atlas") answers general questions about TRT using public clinical guidelines (Endocrine Society Clinical Practice Guidelines, AUA TRT guidelines, AAFP monograph, Bhasin 2018, TRAVERSE trial, FDA prescribing information, peer-reviewed studies) and explicitly redirects diagnostic and dosing questions to your prescriber.
