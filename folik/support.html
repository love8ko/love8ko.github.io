Frequently Asked Questions

How does Folik work?

Folik combines three things: (1) a 5-angle hair photo scan that estimates relative density and hairline stage (Norwood / Ludwig); (2) a sourced AI Coach grounded in AAD, ISHRS, FDA, Mayo, Cleveland, Endocrine Society, and 50+ PubMed extracts — every answer cites its source; (3) a treatment log so you can correlate what you're doing with what you're seeing in your photos.

Is Folik a medical device?

No. Folik is a Health & Fitness wellness companion, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. Always consult a dermatologist before starting, changing, or stopping any treatment. See the Medical Disclaimer.

How accurate is the hair density estimate?

The estimate is an AI approximation based on photo analysis. Lighting, angle, hair length, and styling can affect it significantly. The most reliable signal is the relative trend over multiple consistent scans (same lighting, same angles), not any single number. Folik shows a confidence score with each scan.

How do I take great scans?
  • Same time of day, every time
  • Natural daylight, indirect (no direct sun)
  • Hair clean, dry, off the forehead
  • Phone at eye level for Front; flat over your head for Top; mirror or another person for Crown
  • Same haircut length when possible
What does the AI Coach actually know?

The Coach answers from an on-device knowledge base (~11MB) of published guidelines and peer-reviewed extracts: AAD clinical guidelines, ISHRS hair restoration guidelines, FDA drug labels (minoxidil, finasteride, dutasteride), Mayo Clinic and Cleveland Clinic patient guides, Endocrine Society guidelines (for hormonal context), and 50+ PubMed studies. Every reply includes a tap-able citation. The Coach refuses to answer if the knowledge base doesn't have grounding for your question — no hallucinations.

Can the AI Coach prescribe a treatment?

No. The Coach explains how published guidelines describe treatments (e.g., "FDA-approved minoxidil 5% topical applied twice daily for male pattern hair loss") but does not prescribe, recommend a specific dose, or replace a dermatologist visit. Always confirm with a qualified prescriber.

Why does Folik ask for my gender during onboarding?

Hair loss patterns differ by sex hormone profile (Norwood scale for male pattern, Ludwig scale for female pattern). The persona lanes (Linda doctor-dismissed, Sarah postpartum, Maya stress shed, etc.) personalize Coach prompts and treatment relevance. You can also Skip — that shows the full lane list.

Is my data private?

Yes. Photos, treatment log, chat history, and analysis results are stored locally on your device. Photos are sent to our AI provider for analysis only and are not retained after processing. We do not collect personal information, do not use advertising SDKs, and do not run analytics platforms. See the full Privacy Policy.

Are my hair photos used for face recognition?

No. The 5-angle scan is for hair analysis (density, stage). Front and side angles incidentally contain your face, but Folik does not perform face recognition, biometric template extraction, identity matching, or any facial analytics. See Privacy Policy §1.2.

Tips for Best Results

  • Consistency > perfection: the SAME conditions every scan beat better-but-different conditions
  • Track the trend, not the number: a single density estimate is noisy; week-over-week trend is the signal
  • Photograph treatments too: log start dates and frequencies — Folik correlates them with your density chart
  • Bring the data to your derm: a 90-day photo strip is more useful than "I think it's getting worse"
