What Folik is not

Folik is educational, not medical advice. Folik is not a medical device, does not diagnose, and does not prescribe. AI Coach is forbidden from recommending specific drug doses or telling users to start, stop, or switch a prescription. Always consult a board-certified dermatologist before starting or changing any treatment, including over-the-counter products like minoxidil. Read the full Medical Disclaimer.
