About the App

JewelSnap is an AI-powered jewelry identification and appraisal app for iPhone. Simply take a photo of any piece of jewelry — a ring, necklace, bracelet, brooch, earrings, or watch — and receive an instant analysis including the jewelry type, materials, gemstones, era, estimated market value, authenticity assessment, and stolen property check. Build your personal jewelry collection portfolio with total value tracking, and consult with an AI jewelry expert for deeper insights.

Getting Started

Your First Scan

  1. Open JewelSnap and tap the camera button on the Scan screen
  2. Position your jewelry item in good lighting against a clean, contrasting background
  3. Take a photo, or tap the gallery icon to select an existing photo
  4. Wait a few seconds while the AI analyzes your piece
  5. Review your results: type, materials, stones, era, value, and authenticity
  6. Tap "Save to Collection" to add it to your portfolio
Tip for best results: Use natural daylight or bright indoor lighting. Place jewelry on a dark, non-reflective surface (dark fabric or velvet works best). Capture the entire piece in the frame with as much detail visible as possible. For rings, photograph from a slight angle to show the setting and stone.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI identification?

JewelSnap uses advanced AI vision technology trained to recognize thousands of jewelry types, materials, and gemstones. Accuracy depends on photo quality, lighting, and the complexity of the piece. For common jewelry types (gold rings, diamond solitaires, pearl necklaces), accuracy is very high. For unusual, antique, or highly specialized pieces, the AI provides its best assessment with a confidence level indicator. Always consult a certified appraiser for high-stakes decisions.

Can JewelSnap determine if my jewelry is real or fake?

The App provides an authenticity assessment based on visual characteristics — including craftsmanship quality, material appearance, stone characteristics, hallmark visibility, and stylistic consistency. It assigns a confidence level (High / Medium / Low) to its verdict. However, visual analysis alone cannot replace laboratory testing (acid tests, thermal conductivity, spectroscopy). For definitive authentication, visit a certified gemologist.

How does the value estimation work?

The AI estimates a market value range based on the identified materials, stones, size, era, condition, and current market trends for similar pieces. The estimate represents a typical retail or secondary market range — not an insurance value or liquidation value. Actual value can vary significantly based on factors not visible in photos (weight, stone quality grades, provenance, maker marks on the back, etc.).

What is the Stolen Property Check?

The premium stolen property check compares your jewelry's characteristics against known stolen property databases including the Art Loss Register, Interpol's Works of Art database, and FBI's National Stolen Art File. A "No Match Found" result means no similar items were found in these databases. This is an informational tool and does not constitute a legal clearance certificate.

Does the app work offline?

The scanning and AI chat features require an internet connection, as the AI analysis is performed by a cloud-based service. However, your saved collection and all previously scanned items are available offline. You can browse your collection, view past scan results, and see your portfolio value without any internet connection.

What types of jewelry can I scan?

JewelSnap can identify virtually any type of jewelry including:

  • Rings (engagement, wedding, cocktail, signet, class rings)
  • Necklaces and pendants (chains, chokers, lariats, statement pieces)
  • Bracelets (bangles, tennis bracelets, charm bracelets, cuffs)
  • Earrings (studs, hoops, drops, chandeliers, climbers)
  • Brooches and pins
  • Watches (luxury, vintage, dress watches)
  • Loose gemstones
  • Costume and fashion jewelry
  • Antique and estate jewelry

Can I scan multiple items at once?

For best results, scan one piece of jewelry at a time. The AI focuses on a single item per photo to provide the most accurate identification. If multiple items are in the frame, the AI will typically identify the most prominent piece.

What languages does the AI chat support?

The AI jewelry expert can communicate in most major languages. Simply write your question in your preferred language, and the expert will respond in the same language. Supported languages include English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, and many more.

Tips for Better Results

Photography Tips

  • Lighting: Use natural daylight or bright, even indoor lighting. Avoid harsh direct sunlight that creates strong shadows or overexposed reflections on metals.
  • Background: Place jewelry on a plain, dark surface (black velvet, dark fabric, or dark paper). This helps the AI distinguish the piece from its surroundings.
  • Distance: Get close enough to fill most of the frame with the jewelry piece, but keep the entire item visible.
  • Focus: Tap on the jewelry in the camera viewfinder to ensure it's in sharp focus. Blurry photos produce less accurate results.
  • Angle: For rings, a slight 3/4 angle shows both the band and the setting. For necklaces, lay them flat in their natural shape. For brooches, photograph straight-on.
  • Reflections: Minimize reflections on polished metals by adjusting the angle. A slight tilt often eliminates glare from overhead lights.

Getting Better Value Estimates

  • Photograph hallmarks or stamps if visible (14K, 750, 925, etc.) — these help the AI determine exact material purity
  • Include a size reference if possible (a coin next to the piece helps gauge scale)
  • Use the AI Chat to mention any additional details: weight, provenance, receipts, or certificates you may have
  • Scan the same piece from multiple angles for a more comprehensive assessment

Troubleshooting

The camera won't open

Go to your device's Settings → JewelSnap and ensure Camera access is enabled. If it was previously denied, toggle it on and reopen the App.

Scan is taking too long

AI analysis typically takes 3–8 seconds depending on your internet connection. If a scan seems stuck:

  1. Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular data)
  2. Close and reopen the App
  3. Try again with a smaller/clearer photo

Results seem inaccurate

Photo quality is the biggest factor in accuracy. Try the following:

  • Use better lighting — dim photos produce less accurate results
  • Get closer to fill the frame
  • Ensure the photo is in focus
  • Use a contrasting background (avoid placing gold jewelry on yellow or beige surfaces)
  • Try scanning from a different angle

If results still seem off, use the AI Chat to provide additional context — the expert can refine its assessment based on details you share.

I can't access premium features

If you've subscribed but premium features appear locked:

  1. Go to Settings within the App and tap "Restore Purchases"
  2. Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the purchase
  3. Check your subscription status in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  4. Try closing and reopening the App
  5. If the issue persists, contact us at the email below

The App won't install or update

JewelSnap requires iOS 17.0 or later. Check your iOS version in Settings → General → About. If your device supports iOS 17 but you're on an older version, update via Settings → General → Software Update.
