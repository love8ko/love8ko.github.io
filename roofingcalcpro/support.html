About the App

RoofingCalc Pro is a professional roofing calculator for iOS. It includes four calculators: Roof Area & Materials, Pitch, Rafter Length, and Job Cost Estimator. All calculations are saved locally and can be exported as PDF reports.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch between Imperial and Metric?

Use the Units toggle at the top of the Roof Area and Rafter Length calculators.

How do I export a PDF report?

Save a calculation to History, then tap the document icon next to any saved entry to generate and share a PDF.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

How do I delete saved calculations?

Go to the History tab. Tap the trash icon to clear all entries, or swipe left on individual entries to delete them.
