Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 7, 2026

RoofingCalc Pro ("the App") is developed by love8ko. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations and saved history are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data ever leaves your device.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. There are no third-party services integrated into the App.

PDF Reports

PDF reports are generated locally on your device. When you share a report, it is sent directly from your device using the iOS share sheet. The developer does not have access to any reports you create or share.

Third-Party Services

The App does not connect to any external servers or third-party services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
