Privacy Policy

WatchSnap: Watch Identifier

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Overview

WatchSnap is an AI-powered luxury watch identification and appraisal app. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal data.

Photos

When you scan a watch, the photo is sent to our AI service for real-time analysis. Photos are processed immediately and are not stored on any server. No images are retained after analysis is complete.

AI Chat

Chat messages are sent to our AI service for processing. Messages are not stored or retained after the conversation ends.

Local Storage

Your watch collection and scan history are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

WatchSnap does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

WatchSnap does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
