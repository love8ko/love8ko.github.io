Frequently Asked Questions

How does WatchSnap work?

Take a photo of any watch and our AI will identify the brand, model, reference number, estimate the year, check authenticity markers, and provide a current market value.

How many free scans do I get?

You get 3 free scans. After that, a WatchSnap Pro subscription is required for unlimited scans.

Is the AI authentication reliable?

WatchSnap provides AI-generated estimates for informational purposes. Results are not a substitute for professional authentication. Always consult a certified watchmaker for high-value purchases.

Does it work with photos from eBay or Chrono24?

Yes. You can scan watch photos from any source — use the photo gallery icon to select an image.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > WatchSnap Pro > Cancel Subscription.

Does it work offline?

No. WatchSnap requires an internet connection for AI analysis.

What iOS version is required?

WatchSnap requires iOS 17.0 or later.
