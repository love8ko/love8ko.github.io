3.5 Anti-patterns · что НЕ показываем на App Store

⛔ DO NOT — banned forever (Apple §1.4.1 + compliance + UX safety)

Если видишь в макете — переделывай. Без исключений.

  • Words «safe / unsafe / dangerous / harmful» на TITLE / SUB / inside-phone copy — banned per COMPLIANCE.md. Allowed: «Flagged in research» · «Not flagged» · «Educational reference».
  • «Doctor recommends» / «Medically approved» claims — App Review §1.4.1 violation. Allowed: «Cited from ACOG / NIH / EWG».
  • «Will protect your baby» / outcome promises — outcome language banned. Allowed: «Trimester-aware verdicts».
  • Explicit belly close-up / «pregnant hand on bump» stock poses — clichéd + anxiety-inducing. Use implied silhouette · product hands · environment.
  • Stoplight ALARM red (#FF3B30 / pure red) — triggers panic, anxious audience опасно. Use muted #D95B5B.
  • Fake stars / fake user count: «★★★★★ 4.9 · 10K users» когда мы ещё <50 reviews. App Review-bait, banned forever.
  • Side-by-side competitor comparison (Little Bean / MommyMeds named) — Apple §1.4.5 (no disparaging competitors).
  • Brand logos as endorsement (Sephora · Ulta · La Roche-Posay) — fair use в screenshot props OK, но не «Recommended by Sephora» style claim.
  • Streaks / badges / XP — gamification банится в reference tool. Беременные ≠ Duolingo. (Принцип №8.)
  • Drop shadows / gradient text / outline на typography — solid weight = trust. Health-adjacent category, не lifestyle.
  • Real ingredient label close-up с identifiable brand-trademark (Olay logo full-frame) — IP risk. Use mocked-up label или generic packaging.
  • Disclaimer пропущен на Slot 1 / 5 / 6 (medical-adjacent slots) — «Discuss with your OB-GYN» footer обязателен.
