Privacy Policy

BumpCheck: Pregnancy Ingredient Reference

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Summary: BumpCheck stores all your scan history and preferences locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal health information. No account is required.

1. Overview

BumpCheck is an educational reference app for expecting and breastfeeding mothers. It provides information about ingredients in cosmetics, medications, and food based on publicly available research. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Your privacy is important to us.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only using Apple's SwiftData framework and never leaves your device:

  • Scan history (ingredients, product names, verdicts, timestamps)
  • Favorites list
  • Your pregnancy or breastfeeding stage preference (TTC, T1, T2, T3, Postpartum, Breastfeeding)
  • Onboarding completion status and disclaimer acceptance
  • Weekly scan quota counter

No account is required. No user profile, name, email, or identifying information is collected.

3. Data Sent to External Services

BumpCheck connects to the following services to provide core functionality:

  • OpenAI API (via our Cloudflare Worker proxy) — for ingredient extraction from photos you scan. Only the cropped label image is sent. Images are not stored after processing. Queries are not linked to your identity.
  • DeepSeek API (via our Cloudflare Worker proxy) — for educational verdict reasoning on flagged ingredients. Only the ingredient name and your stage selection are sent.
  • NIH RxNav API (rxnav.nlm.nih.gov) — to look up medications and potential interactions. Only the medication name is sent.
  • NIH LactMed API (wsearch.nlm.nih.gov) — to reference breastfeeding compatibility information. Only the substance name is sent.
  • Open Beauty Facts & Open Food Facts — public product barcode databases. Only the barcode is sent.

No personal information, device identifier, or pregnancy stage is ever sent to these services.

4. Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are managed by RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service. RevenueCat receives:

  • An anonymous device identifier generated by RevenueCat
  • Subscription status (active, expired, trial)
  • Product identifier of purchased tier

RevenueCat does not receive any health information, scan history, or ingredient data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Analytics and Tracking

BumpCheck does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs.

Only Apple's built-in crash diagnostics are collected, which you can disable in your device Settings under Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

6. Children's Privacy

BumpCheck is intended for adults (age 17+ in line with App Store guidelines for pregnancy-related content). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Health Data Disclaimer

BumpCheck provides educational information from public health sources (CDC, ACOG, NIH LactMed, NIH RxNav, EWG Skin Deep, CIR, FDA) for informational purposes only. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or health condition. BumpCheck does not provide medical advice.

Always consult your OB-GYN or a qualified healthcare professional before starting, stopping, or changing any product, medication, supplement, or cosmetic during pregnancy or breastfeeding.

8. Data Deletion

Since all scan history and preferences are stored locally on your device, deleting the BumpCheck app from your device removes all your data permanently. There is no server-side data to request deletion of. Your RevenueCat subscription anonymous ID resets when you reinstall.

9. Regional Compliance

BumpCheck complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area and with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents. Because we do not collect personal information, most data subject rights (access, rectification, portability) are automatically satisfied — you already have full access on your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Material changes will also be surfaced inside the app.
