Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 8, 2026

PipeMath Pro: Pipe Calculator ("the App") is developed by love8ko. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations and saved history are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data ever leaves your device.

Specifically, the App does not:

  • Require an account, login, or registration
  • Collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Track your location
  • Use cookies or tracking pixels
  • Make any network requests

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. There are no third-party services integrated into the App. The App contains no advertisements of any kind.

Local Storage

Your calculation history is stored locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any third party
  • Is included in your standard iCloud device backup if you have enabled it
  • Can be deleted at any time by removing entries in the History tab or by deleting the App

PDF Reports

PDF reports are generated locally on your device. When you share a report, it is sent directly from your device using the iOS share sheet. The developer does not have access to any reports you create or share.

Third-Party Services

The App does not connect to any external servers or third-party services. It works entirely offline.

Data Security

Because the App collects no data and makes no network connections, there is no risk of data breach from the developer's side. Your local data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App complies with COPPA requirements.

International Users

The App supports both Imperial and Metric units and is available worldwide. Since no personal data is collected, GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), PIPEDA (Canada), and other privacy regulations are satisfied by default.

Your Rights

Since the App does not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on external servers. To delete your local calculation history, use the delete function within the History screen or delete and reinstall the App.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
