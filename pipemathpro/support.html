About the App

PipeMath Pro is a professional pipe calculator for iOS. It includes six calculators: Pipe Offset (simple & rolling), Pipe Sizing, Drain Slope, Pressure Drop, and Fixture Units. All calculations are saved locally and can be exported as PDF reports. The App works 100% offline.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes. PipeMath Pro works 100% offline with no internet connection required. All calculations are performed on your device.

How do I switch between Imperial and Metric?

Use the Imperial/Metric toggle at the top of each calculator. All inputs and results convert automatically.

What angles are supported for pipe offsets?

The four standard fitting angles: 22.5°, 30°, 45°, and 60°. Both simple offsets and rolling offsets are supported.

How do I calculate a rolling offset?

Open the Offset calculator, switch the Type to "Rolling", select your angle, and enter the Offset and Roll distances. The app calculates true offset and travel automatically.

How do I export a PDF report?

Save a calculation to History, then tap the PDF icon next to any saved entry to generate and share a report. Reports include your inputs, results, and project name.

Can I tag calculations by project?

Yes. Each calculator has a "Job" field where you can enter a project name. This name appears in History and on PDF reports.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete saved calculations?

Go to the History tab. Swipe left on individual entries to delete them, or use the trash icon to clear all history.

What iOS version is required?

iOS 17.0 or later. Runs on all iPhones that support iOS 17, including iPhone SE (2nd generation) and later.

Is there a subscription?

No. PipeMath Pro is a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no ads, no in-app purchases.

Formulas

Pipe Offset

Simple: Travel = Offset ÷ sin(angle), Spread = Offset ÷ tan(angle)

Rolling: True Offset = √(offset² + roll²), Travel = True Offset ÷ sin(angle)

Pipe Sizing

D = √(4 × Q ÷ (π × V)), rounded up to nearest standard pipe size.

Drain Slope

Total Drop = Pipe Length × Slope. Code compliance per IPC 704.1 (min 1/8"/ft, recommended 1/4"/ft).

Pressure Drop (Hazen-Williams)

hf = 10.67 × L × Q1.852 ÷ (C1.852 × D4.87)

Troubleshooting

The app won't install

Make sure your iPhone runs iOS 17.0 or later. Check in Settings → General → Software Update.

Calculations seem incorrect

Verify your unit system (Imperial vs Metric) is set correctly. If you believe there is an error, email us with your inputs and expected result.

I lost my calculation history

History is stored locally. If you deleted the app, the history was removed. Export important calculations as PDF before making changes.
