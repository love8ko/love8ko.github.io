CrySnap is a parenting companion app that helps you interpret your baby's cries and provides general parenting guidance via AI chat personas (Pediatric Nurse, Sleep Coach, Lactation Consultant). The cry classifier returns the most likely interpretation of a recording with a confidence score. Voice Mode allows hands-free interaction. Soothing sounds are generated on-device.
