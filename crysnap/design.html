2. Иконка — 4 концепта phase 2 sketch

Каждый концепт — на dark navy bg `#0F1115`. Большой preview = coral вариант. Маленькие справа = тот же концепт в sage `#9CB892`. Финальная palette будет согласована с выбором палитры из раздела 1.

1. C + Waveform Halo

Typographic. Letterform + crescent of sound bars above. Brand letter visible at small sizes.

Pros: Brand letter clear · Familiar pattern · Works at 40×40
Cons: «Just another C app»

2. Speech Bubble + Sound

Chat killer feature
Bubble = chat metaphor. Sound bars inside = cry/voice. Combines killer feature #1 + #2.

Pros: Tells «chat about cry» visually · Differentiates from Nanni/AYA labels · Most aligned с killer #1
Cons: Could read «messaging app» without context

3. Voice Waveform

Pure sound abstraction. Symmetric bell curve = listening + analysis. No letter.

Pros: Unique shape · Voice mode wedge · Could become brand asset
Cons: «Audio app»? Could clash с Spotify/Voice memos perception

4. Heart + Heartbeat

Emotional + medical. Heart shape (warmth) + ECG line (pulse + sound).

Pros: Emotional warmth · Health & Fitness category fit · Recognizable
Cons: Generic «health app» · Many competitors use heart

💡 Все иконки — sketch direction (программно сгенерированы PIL). Финальный icon = professional commission после approval концепта + палитры. Это ranged proof-of-concept чтобы выбрать direction, не финальный asset.
