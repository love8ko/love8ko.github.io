2. Иконка — 4 концепта phase 2 sketch кликни концепт + glow

Каждый концепт — на dark navy bg #0F1115. Кликни на маленький превью под концептом чтобы переключить glow цвет (coral / sage). Главный preview обновится.

1. C + Waveform Halo

Typographic. Letterform + crescent of sound bars above. Brand letter visible at small sizes.

Pros: Brand letter clear · Familiar pattern · Works at 40×40
Cons: «Just another C app»

2. Speech Bubble + Sound

Chat killer feature
Bubble = chat. Sound bars inside = cry/voice. Killer #1 + #2 combo.

Pros: Tells «chat about cry» visually · Differentiates from Nanni labels · Most aligned с killer #1
Cons: Could read «messaging app»

3. Voice Waveform

Pure sound abstraction. Symmetric bell curve = listening + analysis. No letter.

Pros: Unique shape · Voice mode wedge · Brand asset potential
Cons: Could clash с Spotify/Voice memos

4. Heart + Heartbeat

Emotional + medical. Heart shape (warmth) + ECG line (pulse + sound).

Pros: Emotional warmth · Health & Fitness fit
Cons: Generic «health app» · Many use heart

💡 Все иконки — sketch direction (программно сгенерированы PIL). Финальный icon = professional commission после approval концепта + палитры. Это proof-of-concept для выбора direction.
