⚠️ Перед любой генерацией копи прочитай это

Caveat early, caveat often. ® на первом упоминании. AI всегда цитирует источник. ER triggers всегда escalate.

Trademark hygiene · ® always
  • Ozempic® (Novo Nordisk)
  • Wegovy® (Novo Nordisk)
  • Rybelsus® (Novo Nordisk)
  • Saxenda® (Novo Nordisk)
  • Mounjaro® (Eli Lilly)
  • Zepbound® (Eli Lilly)
  • Trulicity® (Eli Lilly)
  • Pattern: "for users on Ozempic®" (НЕ "Ozempic users")
Banned phrases · НИКОГДА
  • "cure", "медицинский класс", "medical-grade"
  • "FDA-approved app" / "Approved by Novo Nordisk"
  • "I diagnose..." / "You have..." (диагностический язык)
  • "Increase your dose" / "Skip your next shot"
  • "This is definitely..." (false certainty)
  • "Replace your doctor" / "Better than endo"
  • Brand logos / brand color mimicry
  • Direct comparison brand efficacy
Required phrases · ВСЕГДА
  • "Educational, not medical advice. Consult prescriber."
  • "Talk to your prescriber"
  • Source citation в AI Nurse ("Per FDA prescribing info...")
  • ER triggers: vomiting>24h, severe abdominal pain (pancreatitis), jaundice, suicidal ideation
  • "Call 911" / "Call 988" для true emergency
  • Trademark disclaimer footer (App Store)
  • "for users on Ozempic®" framing pattern
