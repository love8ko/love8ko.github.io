POEM severity scoring — the standard dermatologists use

Salvora uses POEM (Patient-Oriented Eczema Measure) — the methodology used by dermatologists worldwide. Each scan returns a clear severity band so you always know where you stand:

Clear (0–2) — minimal symptoms, no intervention needed
Mild (3–7) — track triggers, maintain moisturizer routine
Moderate (8–16) — consider treatment plan review with your dermatologist
Severe (17–28) — urgent dermatology consultation recommended

Every screen has a safety strip flagging signs that need urgent care — infection, anaphylaxis, or sudden changes you can't explain.
