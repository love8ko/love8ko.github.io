What VeriBag is — and is not

VeriBag is an independent photo-based reference checklist. It does not provide authentication guarantees, does not issue certificates of authenticity, and does not claim to represent any luxury brand. For high-value transactions we strongly recommend pairing any photo-based reference check with a professional physical authentication service.
